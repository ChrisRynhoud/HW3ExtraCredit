Minecraft is a creative game where the player is allowed to express their creativity as much and in as many ways as they want, even with their friends online!



You can even connect with players all around the world, from U.S. to Japan to Africa and everywhere else!

Minecraft is even used in schools to teach students things such as mathematics in a fun way using the new Minecraft Education Edition in which teachers and their students can interact on their own Minecraft server!

But Minecraft wasnt always this advanced. In its alpha stage minecraft wasnt even 3d.

It was only through the creative genius of the game's original creator a swedish programmer commonly known as, Notch, and his later team that have helped minecraft grow to where it is today.

However, in 2014 Minecraft was purchased by Microsoft for a whopping $2.5 Billion Dollars USD! Now thats a lot of money, huh?

Microsoft has now expanded the Minecraft brand by creating more videogames that take place in the Minecraft universe such as Minecraft Dungeons which came out in 2020 and Minecraft Legends which comes out next year in 2023.

Minecraft has also been a huge influence on myself! Growing up I learned so much about computer technology from developing Minecraft servers and installing Minecraft mods on my computer! It sparked a passion for software engineering inside me! Not to mention all the fun I had playing the game with my friends and my brothers! We still sometimes play the game every now and then and notice just how far its come!
